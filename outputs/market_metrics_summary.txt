## 5. CORE GO-TO-MARKET (GTM) SOLUTIONS & FRAMEWORK
 ➡️ [Tactic A: The Loss-Leader Foot-in-the-Door Strategy]
     Do not pitch high-cost multi-page platforms initially. Offer low-friction landing
     pages with quick-action booking funnels or localized QR-code digital menus.

 ➡️ [Tactic B: Leverage the Network Effect via Review Aggregation]
     Use their high review volumes as structural leverage. Show them how an independent
     domain pulling in live Google API reviews increases local search authority over corporate chains.

 ➡️ [Tactic C: Value-Based Value Pricing]
     Frame the web solution not as an expense, but as a leak-plugging asset. Contrast the cost
     of a simple deployment against the value of lost foot traffic going to competitors with sites.
